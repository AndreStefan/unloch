Safety is Non-Negotiable

We built Unloch on five unbreakable principles.

01

AI is a Messenger

Every AI output traces to a clinician decision. The AI executes the treatment plan — it never creates one.

02

Crisis Detection First

Before any rule matching, on every single message, crisis detection runs. No exceptions.

03

Audit Everything

Immutable logs. No deletions. Every interaction is recorded and reviewable.

04

Patient Initiates

The AI never proactively contacts patients unless explicitly opted in. You're always in control.

05

HIPAA From Day One

Encryption, BAAs, and access controls are foundational — not features added later.
